About Us

Name: Duex Amies (French for " Two Friends")

Founded: 27th June 2005

Founders: Nandini singh, kajal, bhoomika, vaishnavi

Headquarters: Prathamesh society, kalyani Nagar,pune Maharashtra 411006

Brand Concept: Duex Amies is a contemporary clothing brand inspired by the bond of friendship and the celebration of individuality.
The brand aims to create different and stylish pieces that cater to a wide range of tastes, emphasizing comfort and quality. Since 20 years

Target Audience: The brand primarily targets fashion-forward individuals aged 18-35 who appreciate modern design and seek unique pieces that reflect their personal style.

*Like :- college, office's, any function, etc
